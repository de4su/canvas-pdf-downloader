About OPIT PDF Downloader

Version 1.0.0 (Rebuild)

This extension helps you scan OPIT Canvas course modules and quickly download PDF resources.

  • Detects direct PDF/file links in module pages
  • Resolves module attachment links to real download links
  • Finds live session/recording links
  • Keeps download history to avoid duplicate downloads
